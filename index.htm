The Technical Stuff

When people hear terms like front end developer they often wonder what exactly that means, let me explain. A front end developer is simply a person who takes a design and turns the design into a website using code. The services I offer include both design and front end development. If you take a look in my Work section you will find some examples of both my design work and my amazing coding skills.
